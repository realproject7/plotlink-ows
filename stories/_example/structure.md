# The Last Cartographer

## Core Concept

In a world where digital maps have erased the unknown, Mira Voss is the last person who draws maps by hand. She works in the basement of a crumbling university archive, cataloguing forgotten cartographic artifacts. When she discovers a hand-drawn map that shouldn't exist — depicting a landmass that appears on no satellite image, no survey, no database — she's pulled into a conspiracy that suggests the world is larger than anyone alive remembers.

## Main Characters

### Mira Voss
- **Age**: 32
- **Personality**: Methodical, quietly stubborn, finds comfort in precision. Talks to herself when thinking. Prefers paper to screens.
- **Flaw**: Avoids risk to the point of paralysis. Uses routine as armor against uncertainty.
- **Arc**: From someone who maps what exists to someone who maps what's possible.

### Hadley Crowe
- **Age**: 58
- **Personality**: Mira's former mentor. Warm but evasive. Knows more than he lets on.
- **Flaw**: Has spent decades keeping a secret that was never his to keep.
- **Arc**: From protector of secrets to reluctant guide into the unknown.

### Jin-seo Park
- **Age**: 27
- **Personality**: A satellite imagery analyst who finds the anomaly from the other end — a gap in the data where there shouldn't be one.
- **Flaw**: Impulsive. Acts before thinking. Trusts technology over people.
- **Arc**: From data purist to someone who accepts that not everything can be measured.

## Story Arc

**Beginning**: Mira finds the impossible map in the archive. It's hand-drawn, undated, and depicts a landmass between Iceland and Norway that doesn't exist. She tries to dismiss it but can't.

**Middle**: Jin-seo independently discovers a satellite dead zone in the same coordinates. They connect. Hadley warns them to stop looking. Someone breaks into the archive. The map is stolen — but Mira has already memorized it.

**End**: They discover that the landmass exists but has been systematically erased from every digital system for decades. The question shifts from "is it real?" to "who erased it, and why?"

## Chapter Plan

1. **Genesis** — Hook: the impossible map. Mira's world of order meets something that shouldn't exist.
2. **Plot 01 — The Blank Spot** — Mira examines the map. Tries to debunk it. Fails. Contacts Hadley.
3. **Plot 02 — Dead Pixels** — Jin-seo's POV. She finds the satellite gap. Her supervisor tells her to ignore it.
4. **Plot 03 — The Archive Burns** — Break-in at the archive. Map stolen. Mira and Jin-seo meet.
5. **Plot 04 — What Hadley Knows** — Hadley reveals he's seen the map before. Decades ago. With different coordinates.
6. **Plot 05 — Coordinates** — They piece together that the landmass moves. Or the maps do.
